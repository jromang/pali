9.2.1.5 Satipaṭṭhāna
Asaṅkhatasaṃyutta
Dutiyavagga
Asaṅkhatasutta
Satipaṭṭhāna
1700Katamo ca, bhikkhave, asaṅkhatagāmimaggo? Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu kāye kāyānupassī viharati ātāpī sampajāno satimā vineyya loke abhijjhādomanassaṃ. Ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, asaṅkhatagāmimaggo…pe…  katamo ca, bhikkhave, asaṅkhatagāmimaggo? Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu vedanāsu vedanānupassī viharati…pe…  ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, asaṅkhatagāmimaggo…pe…  katamo ca, bhikkhave, asaṅkhatagāmimaggo? Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu citte cittānupassī viharati…pe…  ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, asaṅkhatagāmimaggo…pe…  katamo ca, bhikkhave, asaṅkhatagāmimaggo? Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu dhammesu dhammānupassī viharati…pe…  ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, asaṅkhatagāmimaggo…pe… . (1.9--12)
