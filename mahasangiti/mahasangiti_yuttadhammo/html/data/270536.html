1.4.4.4 Adhiṭṭhānaṭṭhaniddesa
Mahāvagga
Indriyakathā
Catutthasuttantaniddesa
Adhiṭṭhānaṭṭhaniddesa
202.
1083Kathaṃ adhiṭṭhānaṭṭhena indriyāni daṭṭhabbāni? Saddhindriyassa bhāvanāya chando uppajjati—  chandavasena saddhāvasena saddhindriyaṃ adhiṭṭhāti. Chandavasena pāmojjaṃ uppajjati—  pāmojjavasena saddhāvasena saddhindriyaṃ adhiṭṭhāti…pe…  evaṃ adhiṭṭhānaṭṭhena indriyāni daṭṭhabbāni.
