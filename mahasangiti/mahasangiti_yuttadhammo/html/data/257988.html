10 Kusalattika, Sekkhattika
Kusalattika, Sekkhattika
14.
51Nakusalaṃ nasekkhaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā kusalo sekkho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha…pe…  āsevane tīṇi…pe…  vipāke tīṇi…pe…  avigate cha.
52Nakusalaṃ naasekkhaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā abyākato asekkho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
53Nakusalaṃ nanevasekkhanāsekkhaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato nevasekkhanāsekkho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā pañca, adhipatiyā pañca…pe…  vipāke tīṇi…pe…  avigate pañca.
