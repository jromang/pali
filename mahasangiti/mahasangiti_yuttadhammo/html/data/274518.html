1.4.2 Dutiyapaṭipattisutta
Maggasaṃyutta
Paṭipattivagga
Dutiyapaṭipattisutta
32.
119Sāvatthinidānaṃ. “Micchāpaṭipannañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi, sammāpaṭipannañca. Taṃ suṇātha. Katamo ca, bhikkhave, micchāpaṭipanno? Idha, bhikkhave, ekacco micchādiṭṭhiko hoti…pe…  micchāsamādhi—  ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, micchāpaṭipanno. Katamo ca, bhikkhave, sammāpaṭipanno? Idha, bhikkhave, ekacco sammādiṭṭhiko hoti…pe…  sammāsamādhi—  ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, sammāpaṭipanno”ti.
120Dutiyaṃ.
