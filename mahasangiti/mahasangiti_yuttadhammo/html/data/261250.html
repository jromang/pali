10.4.5.1--4 Paccayānulomādi
Mahantaraduka
Cittasampayuttaduka
Saṃsaṭṭhavāra
Paccayānulomādi
162.
2712Cittasampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ saṃsaṭṭho cittasampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  cittasampayuttaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ saṃsaṭṭhā dve khandhā, dve khandhe…pe…  paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe… .
2713Hetuyā ekaṃ, ārammaṇe ekaṃ, adhipatiyā ekaṃ (sabbattha ekaṃ), avigate ekaṃ.
2714Nahetuyā ekaṃ, naadhipatiyā ekaṃ, napurejāte ekaṃ, napacchājāte ekaṃ, naāsevane ekaṃ, nakamme ekaṃ, navipāke ekaṃ, najhāne ekaṃ, namagge ekaṃ, navippayutte ekaṃ.
