11.
2644Atīto dhammo paccuppannassa dhammassa āsevanapaccayena paccayo—  purimā purimā atītā khandhā pacchimānaṃ pacchimānaṃ paccuppannānaṃ khandhānaṃ āsevanapaccayena paccayo. Anulomaṃ gotrabhussa…  anulomaṃ vodānassa…  gotrabhu maggassa…  vodānaṃ maggassa āsevanapaccayena paccayo. (1)
