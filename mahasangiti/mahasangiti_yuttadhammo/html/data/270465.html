40.1.8.12 Sūcigharānisaṃsa
Pilindavacchavagga
Pilindavacchattheraapadāna
Dānānisaṃsakathā
Sūcigharānisaṃsa
123.
4420
Sūcighare sugate datvā,
saṃghe gaṇavaruttame;
Tīṇānisaṃse anubhomi,
kammānucchavike mama.
124.
4421
Cetosukhaṃ kāyasukhaṃ,
iriyāpathajaṃ sukhaṃ;
Ime guṇe paṭilabhe,
tassa nissandato ahaṃ.
