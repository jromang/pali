1056Noāsavo dhammo noāsavassa dhammassa indriyapaccayena paccayo—  noāsavā indriyā sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ indriyapaccayena paccayo; paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe…  cakkhundriyaṃ cakkhuviññāṇassa…pe…  kāyindriyaṃ kāyaviññāṇassa…pe… . Rūpajīvitindriyaṃ—  kaṭattārūpānaṃ indriyapaccayena paccayo…  tīṇi…  jhānapaccayena paccayo…  tīṇi…  maggapaccayena paccayo…  nava…  sampayuttapaccayena paccayo…  nava.
