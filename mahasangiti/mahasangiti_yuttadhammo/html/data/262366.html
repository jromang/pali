Nahetu
260.
823Akusalaṃ dhammaṃ sampayutto akusalo dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā—  vicikicchāsahagate uddhaccasahagate khandhe sampayutto vicikicchāsahagato uddhaccasahagato moho. (1)
261.
824Abyākataṃ dhammaṃ sampayutto abyākato dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā—  ahetukaṃ vipākābyākataṃ kiriyābyākataṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ sampayuttā tayo khandhā, tayo khandhe sampayutto eko khandho, dve khandhe sampayuttā dve khandhā; ahetukapaṭisandhikkhaṇe vipākābyākataṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ sampayuttā tayo khandhā, tayo khandhe sampayutto eko khandho, dve khandhe sampayuttā dve khandhā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) (1)
