1222Aṭṭhavidhena rūpasaṅgaho— 
1223Cakkhuviññeyyaṃ rūpaṃ, sotaviññeyyaṃ rūpaṃ, ghānaviññeyyaṃ rūpaṃ, jivhāviññeyyaṃ rūpaṃ, kāyaviññeyyaṃ rūpaṃ, atthi sukhasamphassaṃ, atthi dukkhasamphassaṃ, manodhātuviññeyyaṃ rūpaṃ, manoviññāṇadhātuviññeyyaṃ rūpaṃ.
1224Evaṃ aṭṭhavidhena rūpasaṅgaho.
Aṭṭhakaṃ.
