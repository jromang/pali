36.
95Hetu dhammo hetussa dhammassa indriyapaccayena paccayo…pe… . (Hetumūlake tīṇi.)
96Nahetu dhammo nahetussa dhammassa indriyapaccayena paccayo—  nahetū indriyā sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ indriyapaccayena paccayo; paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe…  cakkhundriyaṃ cakkhuviññāṇassa…pe…  kāyindriyaṃ kāyaviññāṇassa…pe… . Rūpajīvitindriyaṃ—  kaṭattārūpānaṃ indriyapaccayena paccayo. (Evaṃ indriyapaccayā vitthāretabbā. Nava.)
