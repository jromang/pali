30.
847Dassanena pahātabbaṃ…pe…  navippayuttapaccayā—  arūpe dassanena pahātabbaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca…pe… . (1)
848Bhāvanāya pahātabbaṃ…pe…  navippayuttapaccayā—  arūpe bhāvanāya pahātabbaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ…pe… . (1)
849Nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbaṃ…pe…  navippayuttapaccayā—  arūpe nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca…pe…  bāhiraṃ…  āhārasamuṭṭhānaṃ…  utusamuṭṭhānaṃ…  asaññasattānaṃ…pe… . (1)
