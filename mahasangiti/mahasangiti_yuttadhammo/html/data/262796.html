2886Asaṃkiliṭṭhasaṃkilesiko dhammo asaṃkiliṭṭhasaṃkilesikassa dhammassa indriyapaccayena paccayo—  asaṃkiliṭṭhasaṃkilesikā indriyā sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ indriyapaccayena paccayo; paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe…  cakkhundriyaṃ cakkhuviññāṇassa…pe…  kāyindriyaṃ kāyaviññāṇassa indriyapaccayena paccayo. Rūpajīvitindriyaṃ—  kaṭattārūpānaṃ indriyapaccayena paccayo.
