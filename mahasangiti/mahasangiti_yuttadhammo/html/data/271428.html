2.3.2.13.5 Savitakkaduka
Niddesa
Nikkhepakaṇḍa
Dukanikkhepa
Piṭṭhiduka
Savitakkaduka
1275.
1976Katame dhammā savitakkā? Savitakkabhūmiyaṃ kāmāvacare rūpāvacare apariyāpanne, vitakkaṃ ṭhapetvā, taṃsampayutto vedanākkhandho…pe…  viññāṇakkhandho—  ime dhammā savitakkā.
1276.
1977Katame dhammā avitakkā? Avitakkabhūmiyaṃ kāmāvacare rūpāvacare arūpāvacare apariyāpanne; vedanākkhandho…pe…  viññāṇakkhandho; vitakko ca, sabbañca rūpaṃ, asaṅkhatā ca dhātu—  ime dhammā avitakkā.
