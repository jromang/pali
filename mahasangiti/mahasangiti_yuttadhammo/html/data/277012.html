1.2.3.3 Dutiyasamudayasutta
Khandhasaṃyutta
Majjhimapaṇṇāsaka
Khajjanīyavagga
Dutiyasamudayasutta
75.
383Sāvatthinidānaṃ. “Sutavā, bhikkhave, ariyasāvako rūpassa samudayañca atthaṅgamañca assādañca ādīnavañca nissaraṇañca yathābhūtaṃ pajānāti. Vedanāya…  saññāya…  saṅkhārānaṃ…  viññāṇassa samudayañca atthaṅgamañca assādañca ādīnavañca nissaraṇañca yathābhūtaṃ pajānātī”ti.
384Tatiyaṃ.
