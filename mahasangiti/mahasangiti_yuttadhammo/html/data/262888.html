838Dassanena pahātabbaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca dassanena pahātabbo dhammo uppajjati nakammapaccayā—  dassanena pahātabbe khandhe paṭicca dassanena pahātabbā cetanā. (1)
839Bhāvanāya pahātabbaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca bhāvanāya pahātabbo dhammo uppajjati nakammapaccayā—  bhāvanāya pahātabbe khandhe paṭicca bhāvanāya pahātabbā cetanā. (1)
