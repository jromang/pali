195Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ namicchattaniyataṃ dhammaṃ paccayā anidassanaappaṭigho micchattaniyato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
196Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ nasammattaniyataṃ dhammaṃ paccayā anidassanaappaṭigho sammattaniyato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
197Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ naaniyataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca sanidassanasappaṭigho aniyato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekavīsa, adhipatiyā ekavīsa…pe…  avigate ekavīsa.
