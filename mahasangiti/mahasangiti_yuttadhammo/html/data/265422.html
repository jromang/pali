436.
3422Upādinnaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā anupādinno dhammo uppajjati adhipatipaccayā—  vatthuṃ paccayā anupādinnā khandhā. (1)
3423Anupādinnaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā anupādinno dhammo uppajjati adhipatipaccayā—  anupādinnaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paccayā tayo khandhā cittasamuṭṭhānañca rūpaṃ…pe…  dve khandhe…pe…  ekaṃ mahābhūtaṃ…pe…  mahābhūte paccayā cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ rūpaṃ upādārūpaṃ. (1)
3424Upādinnañca anupādinnañca dhammaṃ paccayā anupādinno dhammo uppajjati adhipatipaccayā—  anupādinnaṃ ekaṃ khandhañca vatthuñca paccayā tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe ca…pe… . (1) (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
