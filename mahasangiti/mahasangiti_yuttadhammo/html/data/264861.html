910Lokiyo dhammo lokiyassa dhammassa āsevanapaccayena paccayo—  purimā purimā lokiyā khandhā pacchimānaṃ pacchimānaṃ lokiyānaṃ khandhānaṃ āsevanapaccayena paccayo; anulomaṃ gotrabhussa…  anulomaṃ vodānassa āsevanapaccayena paccayo. (1)
911Lokiyo dhammo lokuttarassa dhammassa āsevanapaccayena paccayo—  gotrabhu maggassa…  vodānaṃ maggassa āsevanapaccayena paccayo. (2)
