2.4.2.2.3 Sanidassanaduka
Niddesa
Aṭṭhakathākaṇḍa
Dukaatthuddhāra
Cūḷantaraduka
Sanidassanaduka
1457.
2167Katame dhammā sanidassanā? Rūpāyatanaṃ—  ime dhammā sanidassanā.
1458.
2168Katame dhammā anidassanā? Cakkhāyatanaṃ…pe…  phoṭṭhabbāyatanaṃ, catūsu bhūmīsu kusalaṃ, akusalaṃ, catūsu bhūmīsu vipāko, tīsu bhūmīsu kiriyābyākataṃ, yañca rūpaṃ anidassanaṃ appaṭighaṃ dhammāyatanapariyāpannaṃ, nibbānañca—  ime dhammā anidassanā.
