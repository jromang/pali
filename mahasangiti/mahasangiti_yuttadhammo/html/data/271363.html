2.3.2.2.4 Sappaṭighaduka
Niddesa
Nikkhepakaṇḍa
Dukanikkhepa
Cūḷantaraduka
Sappaṭighaduka
1095.
1764Katame dhammā sappaṭighā? Cakkhāyatanaṃ…pe…  phoṭṭhabbāyatanaṃ—  ime dhammā sappaṭighā.
1096.
1765Katame dhammā appaṭighā? Vedanākkhandho…pe…  viññāṇakkhandho, yañca rūpaṃ anidassanaṃ appaṭighaṃ dhammāyatanapariyāpannaṃ, asaṅkhatā ca dhātu—  ime dhammā appaṭighā.
