721Kusalaṃ dhammaṃ saṃsaṭṭho…pe…  akusalaṃ dhammaṃ…pe…  abyākataṃ dhammaṃ saṃsaṭṭho abyākato dhammo uppajjati navipākapaccayā—  kiriyābyākataṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ.
722(Saṃsaṭṭhavāre paccanīyavibhaṅge nakamme ca navipāke ca paṭisandhi natthi; avasesesu sabbattha atthi.)
