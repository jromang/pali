99Sekkhaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasekkho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Sekkhaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naasekkho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Sekkhaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nanevasekkhanāsekkho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Sekkhaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naasekkho ca nanevasekkhanāsekkho ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. Sekkhaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasekkho ca naasekkho ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (5)
