1731Naupanissayapaccayā nahetupaccayā naārammaṇapaccayā naadhipatipaccayā naanantarapaccayā nasamanantarapaccayā nasahajātapaccayā nissaye tīṇi, purejāte tīṇi, pacchājāte tīṇi, kamme dve, āhāre ekaṃ, indriye ekaṃ, vippayutte pañca, atthiyā satta, avigate satta. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
