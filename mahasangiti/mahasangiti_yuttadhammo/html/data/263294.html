9.
2775Ajjhattaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca ajjhatto dhammo uppajjati naadhipatipaccayā…pe…  (anulomasahajātasadisaṃ, ninnānākaraṇaṃ) naanantarapaccayā…  nasamanantarapaccayā…  naaññamaññapaccayā…  naupanissayapaccayā, napurejātapaccayā—  arūpe ajjhattaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca…pe…  ajjhatte khandhe paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ rūpaṃ. Paṭisandhikkhaṇe ajjhattaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā kaṭattā ca rūpaṃ…pe…  dve khandhe…pe…  khandhe paṭicca vatthu, vatthuṃ paṭicca khandhā. Ekaṃ mahābhūtaṃ paṭicca…pe…  āhārasamuṭṭhānaṃ…  utusamuṭṭhānaṃ…  asaññasattānaṃ…pe… .
10.
2776Bahiddhā dhammaṃ paṭicca bahiddhā dhammo uppajjati napurejātapaccayā—  arūpe bahiddhā ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca…pe…  dve khandhe…pe…  bahiddhā khandhe paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ rūpaṃ. Paṭisandhikkhaṇe (paripuṇṇaṃ) ekaṃ mahābhūtaṃ…pe…  bāhiraṃ…  āhārasamuṭṭhānaṃ…  utusamuṭṭhānaṃ…  asaññasattānaṃ…pe… .
