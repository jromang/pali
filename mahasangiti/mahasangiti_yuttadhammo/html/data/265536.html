56.
3964Saṃkiliṭṭho dhammo saṃkiliṭṭhassa dhammassa anantarapaccayena paccayo—  purimā purimā saṃkiliṭṭhā khandhā pacchimānaṃ pacchimānaṃ saṃkiliṭṭhānaṃ khandhānaṃ anantarapaccayena paccayo. (1)
3965Saṃkiliṭṭho dhammo asaṃkiliṭṭhassa dhammassa anantarapaccayena paccayo—  saṃkiliṭṭhā khandhā vuṭṭhānassa anantarapaccayena paccayo. (2)

3967Asaṃkiliṭṭho dhammo saṃkiliṭṭhassa dhammassa anantarapaccayena paccayo—  āvajjanā saṃkiliṭṭhānaṃ khandhānaṃ anantarapaccayena paccayo. (2)
