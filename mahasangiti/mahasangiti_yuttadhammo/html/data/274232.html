6.1.4 Samphassasutta
Kilesasaṃyutta
Kilesavagga
Samphassasutta
325.
1099Sāvatthinidānaṃ. “Yo, bhikkhave, cakkhusamphassasmiṃ chandarāgo, cittasseso upakkileso. Yo sotasamphassasmiṃ…  yo ghānasamphassasmiṃ…  yo jivhāsamphassasmiṃ…  yo kāyasamphassasmiṃ…  yo manosamphassasmiṃ chandarāgo, cittasseso upakkileso. Yato kho, bhikkhave, bhikkhuno…pe…  abhiññā sacchikaraṇīyesu dhammesū”ti.
1100Catutthaṃ.
