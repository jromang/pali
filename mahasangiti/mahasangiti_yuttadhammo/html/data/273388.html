4.6.1 Adhammakammadvādasaka
Samathakkhandhaka
Tassapāpiyasikā
Adhammakammadvādasaka
208.
861Tīhi, bhikkhave, aṅgehi samannāgataṃ tassapāpiyasikākammaṃ adhammakammañca hoti, avinayakammañca, duvūpasantañca. Asammukhā kataṃ hoti, appaṭipucchākataṃ hoti, appaṭiññāya kataṃ hoti…pe…  adhammena kataṃ hoti, vaggena kataṃ hoti—  imehi kho, bhikkhave, tīhaṅgehi samannāgataṃ tassapāpiyasikākammaṃ adhammakammañca hoti, avinayakammañca, duvūpasantañca.
