49.
2012Parittaṃ dhammaṃ saṃsaṭṭho paritto dhammo uppajjati nakammapaccayā—  paritte khandhe saṃsaṭṭhā parittā cetanā. (1)
2013Mahaggataṃ dhammaṃ saṃsaṭṭho mahaggato dhammo uppajjati nakammapaccayā—  mahaggate khandhe saṃsaṭṭhā mahaggatā cetanā. (1)
2014Appamāṇaṃ dhammaṃ saṃsaṭṭho appamāṇo dhammo uppajjati nakammapaccayā—  kusale appamāṇe khandhe saṃsaṭṭhā appamāṇā cetanā. (1)
