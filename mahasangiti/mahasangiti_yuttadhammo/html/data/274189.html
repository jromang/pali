3.1.16 Nahotitathāgatosutta
Diṭṭhisaṃyutta
Sotāpattivagga
Nahotitathāgatosutta
221.
967Sāvatthinidānaṃ. “Kismiṃ nu kho, bhikkhave, sati, kiṃ upādāya, kiṃ abhinivissa evaṃ diṭṭhi uppajjati—  ‘na hoti tathāgato paraṃ maraṇā’”ti? “Bhagavaṃmūlakā no, bhante, dhammā…pe…  niyato sambodhiparāyano”ti.
968Soḷasamaṃ.
