16.1--7 Paṭiccādivāra
Maggārammaṇattika
Paṭiccādivāra
44.
121Maggārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca namaggārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Maggārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca namaggahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Maggārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca namaggādhipati dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Maggārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca namaggārammaṇo ca namaggādhipati ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. Maggārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca namaggahetuko ca namaggādhipati ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. Maggārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca namaggārammaṇo ca namaggahetuko ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. Maggārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca namaggārammaṇo ca namaggahetuko ca namaggādhipati ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (7) (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
