77.
2503Citto dhammo cittassa dhammassa upanissayapaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇūpanissayo, anantarūpanissayo, pakatūpanissayo…pe… . Pakatūpanissayo—  cittaṃ cittassa upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  tīṇi.
2504Nocitto dhammo nocittassa dhammassa upanissayapaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇūpanissayo, anantarūpanissayo, pakatūpanissayo…pe… . Pakatūpanissayo—  saddhaṃ upanissāya dānaṃ deti…pe…  mānaṃ jappeti, diṭṭhiṃ gaṇhāti; sīlaṃ…pe…  senāsanaṃ upanissāya dānaṃ deti…pe…  saṃghaṃ bhindati; saddhā…pe…  senāsanaṃ saddhāya…pe…  maggassa, phalasamāpattiyā upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (1)
2505Nocitto dhammo cittassa dhammassa upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (Ime dvepi pūretukāmena sabbattha kātabbā, paṭhamagamanasadisaṃ ninnānākaraṇaṃ.)
2506Citto ca nocitto ca dhammā cittassa dhammassa upanissayapaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇūpanissayo, anantarūpanissayo, pakatūpanissayo…pe… . Pakatūpanissayo—  cittañca sampayuttakā ca khandhā cittassa upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  tīṇi.
