86.
145Hetuṃ dukkhāya vedanāya sampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu dukkhāya vedanāya sampayutto dhammo uppajjati naadhipatipaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
87.
146Nahetuyā ekaṃ, naadhipatiyā nava, napacchājāte nava, naāsevane nava, nakamme tīṇi, navipāke nava, najhāne ekaṃ, namagge ekaṃ. (Paccanīyaṃ.)
147Hetupaccayā naadhipatiyā nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ, anulomapaccanīyaṃ.)
148Nahetupaccayā ārammaṇe ekaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ, paccanīyānulomaṃ.)
