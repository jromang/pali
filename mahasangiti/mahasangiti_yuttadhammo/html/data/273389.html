4.6.2 Dhammakammadvādasaka
Samathakkhandhaka
Tassapāpiyasikā
Dhammakammadvādasaka
209.
862Tīhi, bhikkhave, aṅgehi samannāgataṃ tassapāpiyasikākammaṃ dhammakammañca hoti, vinayakammañca, suvūpasantañca. Sammukhā kataṃ hoti, paṭipucchākataṃ hoti, paṭiññāya kataṃ hoti…pe…  dhammena kataṃ hoti, samaggena kataṃ hoti—  imehi kho, bhikkhave, tīhaṅgehi samannāgataṃ tassapāpiyasikākammaṃ dhammakammañca hoti, vinayakammañca, suvūpasantañca.
