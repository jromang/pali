Vippayutta
64.
3980Saṃkiliṭṭho dhammo asaṃkiliṭṭhassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) (1)
3981Asaṃkiliṭṭho dhammo asaṃkiliṭṭhassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) (1)
3982Asaṃkiliṭṭho dhammo saṃkiliṭṭhassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. Purejātaṃ—  vatthu saṃkiliṭṭhānaṃ khandhānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (2)
