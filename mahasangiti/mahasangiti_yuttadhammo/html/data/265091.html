2040Nīvaraṇasampayutto dhammo nīvaraṇasampayuttassa dhammassa anantarapaccayena paccayo—  purimā purimā nīvaraṇasampayuttā khandhā pacchimānaṃ pacchimānaṃ nīvaraṇasampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ anantarapaccayena paccayo. (Mūlaṃ pucchitabbaṃ.) Nīvaraṇasampayuttā khandhā vuṭṭhānassa anantarapaccayena paccayo. (Idha purimā purimāti natthi.) (2)
