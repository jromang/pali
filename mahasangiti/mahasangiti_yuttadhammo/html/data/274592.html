1.14.1 Oghasutta
Maggasaṃyutta
Oghavagga
Oghasutta
172.
335Sāvatthinidānaṃ. “Cattārome, bhikkhave, oghā. Katame cattāro? Kāmogho, bhavogho, diṭṭhogho, avijjogho—  ime kho, bhikkhave, cattāro oghā. Imesaṃ kho, bhikkhave, catunnaṃ oghānaṃ abhiññāya pariññāya parikkhayāya pahānāya…pe…  ayaṃ ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo bhāvetabbo”ti. (Yathā esanā, evaṃ vitthāretabbaṃ.)
336Paṭhamaṃ.
