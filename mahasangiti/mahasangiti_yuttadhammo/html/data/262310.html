215.
714Akusalaṃ dhammaṃ saṃsaṭṭho akusalo dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā—  vicikicchāsahagate uddhaccasahagate khandhe saṃsaṭṭho vicikicchāsahagato uddhaccasahagato moho. (1)
715Abyākataṃ dhammaṃ saṃsaṭṭho abyākato dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā—  ahetukaṃ vipākābyākataṃ kiriyābyākataṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ saṃsaṭṭhā tayo khandhā, tayo khandhe saṃsaṭṭho eko khandho, dve khandhe saṃsaṭṭhā dve khandhā; ahetukapaṭisandhikkhaṇe vipākābyākataṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ saṃsaṭṭhā tayo khandhā, tayo khandhe saṃsaṭṭho eko khandho, dve khandhe saṃsaṭṭhā dve khandhā. (1)
