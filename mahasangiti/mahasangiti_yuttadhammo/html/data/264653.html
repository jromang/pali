11Hetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu dhammo uppajjati ārammaṇapaccayā (rūpaṃ chaḍḍetvā arūpeyeva nava pañhā)…  adhipatipaccayā (paṭisandhi natthi, paripuṇṇaṃ) ekaṃ mahābhūtaṃ paṭicca…pe…  mahābhūte paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ rūpaṃ upādārūpaṃ…  (imaṃ nānaṃ) anantarapaccayā…  samanantarapaccayā…  sahajātapaccayā (sabbe mahābhūtā yāva asaññasattā)…  aññamaññapaccayā…  nissayapaccayā…  upanissayapaccayā…  purejātapaccayā…  āsevanapaccayā (dvīsupi paṭisandhi natthi)…  kammapaccayā…  vipākapaccayā (saṃkhittaṃ)…  avigatapaccayā.
