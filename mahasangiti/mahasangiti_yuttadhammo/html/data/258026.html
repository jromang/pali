48 Sanidassanattika, Ācayagāmittika
Sanidassanattika, Ācayagāmittika
57.
180Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ naācayagāmiṃ dhammaṃ paccayā anidassanaappaṭigho ācayagāmī dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
181Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ naapacayagāmiṃ dhammaṃ paccayā anidassanaappaṭigho apacayagāmī dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
182Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ nanevācayagāmināpacayagāmiṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca sanidassanasappaṭigho nevācayagāmināpacayagāmī dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekavīsa, adhipatiyā ekavīsa…pe…  avigate ekavīsa.
