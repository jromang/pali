3027Anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo anidassanaappaṭighassa dhammassa āsevanapaccayena paccayo—  purimā purimā anidassanaappaṭighā khandhā pacchimānaṃ pacchimānaṃ anidassanaappaṭighānaṃ khandhānaṃ āsevanapaccayena paccayo. Anulomaṃ gotrabhussa…  anulomaṃ vodānassa…  gotrabhu maggassa…  vodānaṃ maggassa āsevanapaccayena paccayo. (1)
