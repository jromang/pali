Cakkhundriyaṃ cakkhuviññāṇassa…pe…  kāyindriyaṃ kāyaviññāṇassa rūpajīvitindriyaṃ noupādā kaṭattārūpānaṃ indriyapaccayena paccayo. (Mūlaṃ kātabbaṃ.)
