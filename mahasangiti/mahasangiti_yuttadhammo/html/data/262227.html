144.
520Kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā kusalo dhammo uppajjati naadhipatipaccayā—  kusalaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paccayā…  tīṇi.
521Akusalaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā…  tīṇi.
522Abyākataṃ dhammaṃ paccayā…pe…  paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe…  (abyākataṃ paripuṇṇaṃ kātabbaṃ). Bāhiraṃ…  āhārasamuṭṭhānaṃ…  utusamuṭṭhānaṃ…  asaññasattānaṃ ekaṃ mahābhūtaṃ paccayā…pe…  cakkhāyatanaṃ paccayā cakkhuviññāṇaṃ…pe…  kāyāyatanaṃ paccayā kāyaviññāṇaṃ, vatthuṃ paccayā vipākābyākatā kiriyābyākatā khandhā.
523Abyākataṃ dhammaṃ paccayā kusalo dhammo uppajjati naadhipatipaccayā, vatthuṃ paccayā kusalā khandhā…pe… .
524(Yathā anulome sahajātapaccayaṃ, evaṃ gaṇetabbaṃ.)
