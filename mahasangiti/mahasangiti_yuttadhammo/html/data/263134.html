77.
2101Paritto dhammo parittassa dhammassa āsevanapaccayena paccayo—  purimā purimā parittā khandhā pacchimānaṃ pacchimānaṃ parittānaṃ khandhānaṃ…pe…  anulomaṃ gotrabhussa, anulomaṃ vodānassa āsevanapaccayena paccayo. (1)
2102Paritto dhammo mahaggatassa dhammassa āsevanapaccayena paccayo—  paṭhamassa jhānassa parikammaṃ tasseva āsevanapaccayena paccayo…pe…  nevasaññānāsaññāyatanassa parikammaṃ tasseva āsevanapaccayena paccayo. Dibbassa cakkhussa parikammaṃ…pe…  anāgataṃsañāṇassa parikammaṃ anāgataṃsañāṇassa āsevanapaccayena paccayo. (2)
2103Paritto dhammo appamāṇassa dhammassa āsevanapaccayena paccayo—  gotrabhu maggassa…  vodānaṃ maggassa āsevanapaccayena paccayo. (3)
2104Mahaggato dhammo mahaggatassa dhammassa āsevanapaccayena paccayo—  purimā purimā mahaggatā khandhā pacchimānaṃ pacchimānaṃ mahaggatānaṃ khandhānaṃ…pe…  āsevanapaccayena paccayo. (1)
